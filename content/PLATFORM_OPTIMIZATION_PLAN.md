### Video Production Guide

| Element | Recommendation | Why |
|---------|---------------|-----|
| **Length** | 2:30 (maximum 3:00) | Upwork recommends 2-3 min; longer videos see 40% drop-off |
| **Setting** | Clean home office, natural light | Shows professionalism without corporate stiffness |
| **Outfit** | Business casual (polo/button-up) | Matches client expectations for technical freelancers |
| **Camera** | Smartphone (landscape, 1080p) | Client-facing quality, not Hollywood production |
| **Audio** | Wireless mic or quiet room | Clear audio > cinematic visuals |
| **Visuals** | Static camera, minimal editing | Focus on your message, not production value |
| **Screen Share** | Optional 10-sec repo tour | Shows real code, builds credibility |
| **Tone** | Confident, friendly, technical | Balance expertise with approachability |

**Recording Tips**:
- Script bullet points, don't memorize word-for-word (sounds natural)
- Smile when introducing yourself (builds trust)
- Pause 2 seconds before/after recording (easier editing)
- Record 3 takes, pick the best one
- Use natural hand gestures (shows confidence)

**Tools**:
- **Recording**: iPhone/Android camera app (landscape mode)
- **Editing**: iMovie (Mac) or CapCut (cross-platform, free)
- **Upload**: Upwork accepts MP4, MOV up to 500MB
