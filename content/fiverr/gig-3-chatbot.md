## Description (Copy-Paste Ready)

**Your website visitors are leaving without converting because nobody is there to answer their questions at 2 AM.** I build AI chatbots that qualify leads, answer FAQs, and hand off to your sales team at exactly the right moment -- with full CRM sync.

**The Problem**

You are losing leads because:
- Live chat only works during business hours
- Generic chatbots frustrate visitors with canned responses
- Leads fall through the cracks -- no CRM logging, no follow-up triggers
- When a conversation shifts topics (e.g., "I want to buy" becomes "actually, I want to sell"), the bot breaks down

**The Solution**

I deploy a multi-agent chatbot system where specialized AI bots handle different conversation types and automatically hand off to each other -- or to a human -- based on real-time intent analysis.

- **Smart handoff at 0.7 confidence** -- conversations transfer seamlessly between specialist bots
- **Full context preservation** -- the next agent knows everything the previous one discussed
- **CRM sync in real-time** -- every conversation creates/updates contacts in GoHighLevel, HubSpot, or Salesforce
- **Lead scoring built in** -- hot, warm, and cold tags applied automatically
- **Circular prevention** -- the system never bounces a customer between bots endlessly

## FAQ (Copy-Paste Ready)

**Q: What platforms can the chatbot integrate with?**
Web widgets (copy/paste embed), WordPress, React, Vue, and any system via REST API. CRM integrations include GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Salesforce, and custom webhooks. Premium includes Zapier integration.

**Q: Can I use this for industries other than real estate?**
Absolutely. The architecture is domain-agnostic. I have deployed variants for legal services, healthcare, e-commerce, and financial services. You tell me the use case and I configure the bot personas, intents, and handoff logic for your industry.

**Q: What happens when the bot cannot answer a question?**
After 2 failed attempts, the bot offers to rephrase, connect to a human agent (Standard and Premium), or take a message for follow-up. Premium includes full human escalation workflows with notification routing.

**Q: How do I measure ROI?**
Standard includes lead scoring so you can track conversion rates. Premium adds a full analytics dashboard with conversation funnels, sentiment analysis, response time trends, and CSV/PDF export. You will know exactly how many leads the bot qualified and converted.

**Q: Is conversation data secure?**
All conversations are encrypted at rest and in transit. I recommend using your own API keys so data stays with your provider. Premium offers on-premise deployment. NDA and HIPAA compliance support available.
