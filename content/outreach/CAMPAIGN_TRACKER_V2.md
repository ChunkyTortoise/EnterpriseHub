## Follow-Up Rules

### Timing

1. **Day 1**: Initial outreach (email + LinkedIn connection request same week)
2. **Day 4**: Follow-up #1 if no response (add specific value)
3. **Day 8**: Final touch (low-commitment offer: "reply send it")
4. **Day 15**: Move to nurture list (no more direct outreach)
5. **Day 30**: Optional nurture email (case study, blog post, or resource)

### Escalation Rules

- **Reply within 4 hours** to any response (during business hours, 9 AM - 6 PM PT)
- **Positive response**: Schedule call within 48 hours. Send calendar link immediately
- **Question/objection**: Answer directly, do not defer to a call unless appropriate
- **"Not now" response**: Thank them, ask permission to follow up in 30/60/90 days
- **No response after full sequence**: Move to monthly nurture (educational content only)

### Stop Rules

- **Unsubscribe request**: Remove immediately from all lists
- **"Not interested" reply**: Thank them, remove from active outreach, keep on annual check-in
- **Bounce**: Verify email, try alternate if available, remove after 2 bounces
- **3 sequences with no response**: Move to passive nurture (annual check-in only)
