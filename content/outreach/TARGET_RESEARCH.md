## Research Best Practices

### Email Finding Tools
- **Hunter.io**: Find verified emails
- **Snov.io**: Email discovery
- **Apollo.io**: Comprehensive contact database
- **ZoomInfo**: B2B contact data
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator**: Direct outreach

### Verification
- **NeverBounce**: Email verification
- **ZeroBounce**: Email validation
- **Clearbit**: Company data enrichment

### Personalization Tips
1. Reference a recent company announcement or product launch
2. Mention a specific pain point relevant to their business
3. Include a relevant case study from their industry
4. Reference their competitive landscape
5. Comment on their LinkedIn activity before reaching out
