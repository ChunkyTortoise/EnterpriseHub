## Response Handling Playbook

### Positive Reply (Interested)

**Within 4 hours**:
1. Thank them for reply
2. Offer 2-3 calendar slots for 30-min discovery call
3. Include Calendly link as backup
4. Set internal reminder for call prep (1 day before)

**Example**:
> Hi [Name], thanks for getting back to me! I'd love to walk you through the architecture. Are you available Tuesday at 2 PM PT or Wednesday at 10 AM PT? Alternatively, here's my Calendly: [link]

---

### Neutral Reply ("Send more info")

**Within 4 hours**:
1. Send link to live Streamlit demo
2. Include 1-page case study PDF (create if doesn't exist)
3. Offer to answer specific questions
4. Set follow-up reminder (3 days out)

**Example**:
> Absolutely! Here's the live demo: [URL]. I also put together a 1-page case study showing the 89% cost reduction: [link]. Any specific questions I can answer?

---

### "Not Now" Reply

**Within 4 hours**:
1. Thank them for transparency
2. Ask permission to follow up in 3/6 months
3. Offer to add to quarterly newsletter (educational, not sales)
4. Remove from active sequence

**Example**:
> Thanks for letting me know, [Name]. Totally understand the timing. Would it be okay if I check back in 3 months, or would you prefer I add you to my quarterly AI engineering newsletter (deep-dives, not sales pitches)?

---

**Action**:
1. Mark as "Closed Lost - No Response" in tracker
2. Move to long-term nurture (6-month check-in)
3. Remove from active sequence
4. Do NOT send additional emails
