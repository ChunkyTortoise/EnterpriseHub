## FAQ for Potential Sponsors

**Q: What happens if I cancel my sponsorship?**
A: You can cancel anytime. You'll retain access to tier benefits through the end of your billing period. No hard feelings!

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade tiers?**
A: Yes, you can change tiers at any time. GitHub will prorate the difference.

**Q: Do you offer custom/one-time sponsorships?**
A: Yes! Contact caymanroden@gmail.com for custom arrangements, one-time contributions, or company-wide sponsorships.

**Q: Will you add features I request?**
A: Feature requests are prioritized based on:
1. Alignment with project roadmap
2. Benefit to the broader community
3. Technical feasibility
4. Sponsor tier (higher tiers get priority)

**Q: Can I sponsor anonymously?**
A: Yes, GitHub allows anonymous sponsorships. Your name won't appear in SPONSORS.md or public acknowledgments.

**Q: What if my company wants to sponsor?**
A: Great! Contact me at caymanroden@gmail.com for:
- Bulk sponsorships
- Custom invoicing
- Logo placement
- Partnership opportunities

- Discord/Slack members (sponsors vs public)
