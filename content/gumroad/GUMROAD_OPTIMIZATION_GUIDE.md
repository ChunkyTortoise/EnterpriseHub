## 1. Bank Account Setup Guide

### Prerequisites
- Government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport)
- Proof of residence (utility bill, bank statement with address)
- Bank account details (account number, routing number/SWIFT)

### Step-by-Step Instructions

#### Step 1: Log into Gumroad
1. Go to [gumroad.com](https://gumroad.com)
2. Log in with chunktort@gmail.com
3. Click profile icon (top-right corner)

#### Step 2: Navigate to Payout Settings
1. Click **Settings** in the dropdown menu
2. Find **Payouts** section in left sidebar
3. Click **Payout Settings** or **Bank Account**

#### Step 3: Enter Bank Details
1. Select your country (United States)
2. Choose **Bank Account** as payout method
3. Enter the following:
   - **Account Holder Name**: Your full legal name (as on ID)
   - **Account Number**: Your bank account number
   - **Routing Number**: 9-digit routing number (US) or SWIFT/BIC (international)
   - **Account Type**: Checking or Savings

#### Step 4: Upload Verification Documents
1. Upload **government-issued photo ID**:
   - Driver's license (front/back)
   - Passport (photo page)
   - State ID card
2. Upload **proof of residence**:
   - Recent utility bill (electric, water, gas)
   - Bank statement with address
   - Lease agreement
   - Government-issued document with address

#### Step 5: Choose Payment Currency
1. Select **USD** (or your preferred currency)
2. Note: Gumroad supports USD, GBP, EUR, CAD, AUD, and others

#### Step 6: Set Payment Threshold (Optional)
1. Default: Weekly payouts (Friday)
2. Optional: Set minimum threshold (e.g., $100 minimum before payout)

#### Step 7: Verify and Save
1. Review all details for accuracy
2. Click **Save Changes**
3. Wait for Gumroad verification (typically 1-3 business days)

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| **"Bank account rejected"** | Verify account number matches bank statement exactly. Contact bank for routing number confirmation. |
| **"ID verification failed"** | Ensure ID photo is clear, all edges visible, no glare. Upload higher resolution image. |
| **"Proof of residence rejected"** | Document must be dated within last 3 months. Name and address must match ID exactly. |
| **"Currency not supported"** | If your country isn't supported for bank deposits, use PayPal as payout method instead. |
| **"Can't find routing number"** | Check bank website, mobile app, or bottom of checks. Call bank support for assistance. |

### Alternative: PayPal Payout Setup

If bank account setup fails or isn't available in your country:

1. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Payouts**
2. Select **PayPal** instead of Bank Account
3. Enter PayPal email address
4. Verify PayPal account is active and unrestricted
5. No ID verification required for PayPal payouts

### Expected Timeline
- **Bank account verification**: 1-3 business days
- **First payout**: Fridays after verification
- **Subsequent payouts**: Weekly (Fridays) or based on threshold
