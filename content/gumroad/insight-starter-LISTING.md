## What You Get

### 30-Second Pipeline
1. Upload CSV or Excel
2. Auto-profiling runs immediately (quality, distributions, correlations)
3. Interactive dashboard appears with filtering and drill-down
4. Export PDF report for stakeholders

### 4 Marketing Attribution Models
- **First-Touch**: 100% credit to first interaction (awareness campaigns)
- **Last-Touch**: 100% credit to last interaction (conversion focus)
- **Linear**: Equal credit across all touchpoints (balanced view)
- **Time-Decay**: More credit to recent interactions (short sales cycles)

### Predictive Modeling with SHAP
Build classification and regression models with one click. Every prediction includes SHAP feature importance explanations -- know WHY the model made each prediction.

### Clustering Analysis
K-means and hierarchical clustering for customer segmentation. Automatically find natural groupings in your data.

### Time Series Forecasting
ARIMA-based forecasting with confidence intervals. Predict future values from historical patterns.

### Anomaly Detection
Statistical and ML-based outlier detection. Find unusual patterns your team would miss.

### Data Cleaning
Missing value imputation, outlier handling, duplicate detection. Clean data without pandas gymnastics.

### PDF Reports
Professional reports with executive summary, visualizations, model results, methodology, and recommendations. Ready for stakeholder presentations.
