# Scale Sprint ($12K-$20K)

## Outcome
Deliver advanced orchestration, custom integrations, and SLA-backed operations.

## Scope
- Advanced orchestration flows and failure handling
- Integration connectors (CRM, comms, analytics)
- SLA instrumentation and alerting setup
- Executive proof-pack and weekly metrics snapshots

## Exclusions
- Global multi-region rollout
- Full self-serve SaaS onboarding portal
- Custom hardware/on-prem enterprise stack

## KPIs
- 99.5%+ availability target for scoped services
- Mean time to detect incidents under 5 minutes
- Weekly proof-pack delivery cadence maintained

## Acceptance Tests
- Performance test for target endpoints
- Observability alerts tested end-to-end
- Deployment and rollback drills documented

## Timeline
- Architecture lock: 3 days
- Build + integration: 10-15 days
- Hardening + handoff: 4-6 days
