# Lead Response Copilot - Statement of Work (Reusable Template)

## 1. Engagement Summary
- Client:
- Package: Lead Response Copilot
- Setup Fee: $2,000 (one-time)
- Recurring Fee: $1,500/month
- Start Date:
- Target Go-Live Date: Within 5 business days of access handoff

## 2. Deliverables
1. Lead intake and qualification workflow automation.
2. CRM sync and handoff reliability setup.
3. Weekly KPI proof-pack generation and review cadence.
4. Compliance and response-SLA monitoring baseline.

## 3. In Scope
- v2 routes used for pilot reporting and operational visibility.
- Tenant-scoped deployment and access controls.
- One launch runbook and one onboarding checklist.

## 4. Out of Scope
- New verticals beyond SMB real-estate teams.
- Full platform modernization outside pilot route set.
- Custom enterprise SSO or legal/compliance frameworks beyond pilot baseline.

## 5. Acceptance Criteria
1. Go-live achieved within 5 business days after client access handoff.
2. Weekly KPI proof-pack delivered and reviewed.
3. v2 route contracts pass with data/meta/error envelope + required headers.
4. Tenant-scope controls enforced for pilot API usage.

## 6. Dependencies
- Client access requirements: CRM admin, API keys, data export/import permission.
- Data requirements: lead history, appointment outcomes, contact metadata.
- Third-party integrations: GoHighLevel, messaging provider, dashboard hosting.

## 7. Renewal and KPI Review
- Weekly KPI review includes:
  - Response SLA attainment (5-minute target)
  - Lead qualification throughput
  - Appointments booked
  - Cost per qualified lead
- Renewal decision is tied to agreed KPI deltas in writing.

## 8. Commercials and Milestones
- Milestone 1 (Kickoff + Access Validation): 25% of setup fee
- Milestone 2 (Configuration + Dry Run): 50% of setup fee
- Milestone 3 (Go-Live + Week 1 Proof-Pack): 25% of setup fee
- Monthly recurring starts at go-live

## 9. Change Control
Any scope additions require written approval and timeline re-estimate.
