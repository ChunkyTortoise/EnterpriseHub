## Proof of Capability

### Relevant Case Studies

#### Real Estate Lead Qualification Automation

**Industry:** Real Estate  
**Duration:** 8 weeks  
**Team Size:** 1

**Problem:** A real estate brokerage was losing 40% of leads due to slow response times. Manual qualification took 45+ minutes per lead.

**Solution:** Built a multi-agent AI system with three specialized bots using a Q0-Q4 qualification framework. Implemented 3-tier Redis caching for 89% cost reduction.

**Outcomes:**
- Lead response time reduced from 45min to 2min (95% improvement)
- $240,000 annual savings from reduced manual review time
- Conversion rate increased from 12% to 28% (133% improvement)
- 3x increase in agent productivity

**Tech Stack:** FastAPI, Claude API, PostgreSQL, Redis, Streamlit, GoHighLevel

**Proof:** [Live Dashboard](https://ct-enterprise-ai.streamlit.app) | [GitHub](https://github.com/ChunkyTortoise/EnterpriseHub)
