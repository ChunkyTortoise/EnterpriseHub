## Usage Tips

1. **Listen First**: Let the client fully express their objection before responding
2. **Empathize Always**: Validate their concern before moving to solutions
3. **Be Specific**: Reference relevant case studies and certifications
4. **Ask Questions**: Use questions to understand the root concern
5. **Offer Alternatives**: Provide options if the initial response doesn't resonate
6. **Follow Up**: Always end with a clear next step or question
