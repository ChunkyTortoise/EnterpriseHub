# Lead Response Copilot - Launch Runbook

## Goal
Launch a pilot tenant safely and deliver first weekly KPI proof-pack within 7 days.

## T-2 Days
1. Verify all required credentials and access.
2. Validate v2 route contracts and tenant scoping.
3. Run dry-run lead intake and handoff simulation.

## T-1 Day
1. Freeze non-critical changes.
2. Confirm monitoring and alert channels.
3. Share launch plan and rollback contacts with client.

## Launch Day
1. Enable production integrations for tenant.
2. Verify first real lead event path end-to-end.
3. Confirm SLA tracking, qualification count, and appointment tracking.
4. Record any launch incidents with correlation IDs.

## T+1 to T+7
1. Generate weekly proof-pack.
2. Review KPI deltas with client.
3. Capture issues and add to prioritized backlog.
