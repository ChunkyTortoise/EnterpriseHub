## PRESENTER TRAINING NOTES

### Key Delivery Tips
1. **Pace**: Slow down during technical demo - let them see it work
2. **Confidence**: Know the numbers cold - no hesitation on ROI claims
3. **Energy**: Maintain enthusiasm but stay professional
4. **Questions**: Encourage questions but keep to 3-minute timeline
5. **Proof**: Always have client references ready to mention

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Rushing through the live demo portion
- Getting too technical on implementation details
- Forgetting to emphasize the guarantee/risk reversal
- Not customizing ROI calculator for their specific business
- Failing to create urgency around implementation timeline

### Success Multipliers
- Use their actual business name in demo examples
- Reference their specific CRM system during integration talk
- Mention their industry challenges specifically
- Show enthusiasm for their potential results
- Always end with clear next steps and timeline
