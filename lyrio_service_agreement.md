## 1. Scope of Work
Provider agrees to deliver technical services as defined in individual Statements of Work (SOW). This may include custom AI middleware, GHL integrations, and strategic consulting.

## 2. Compensation Models (Refer to SOW)
This agreement supports multiple compensation structures:
*   **Fixed-Price:** Payable 50% upfront, 50% on delivery.
*   **Monthly License:** A recurring fee for access to Provider-hosted API services.
*   **Revenue Share:** A monthly payout based on Client's "Premium User" count.

## 3. Intellectual Property (The "Engine" vs. "Output")
*   **Provider IP:** All pre-existing code, libraries, and the "Engine" used to power features remain the sole property of [Your Name].
*   **Client License:** Client is granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the Provider's API for the duration of the paid term.
*   **Deliverables:** Any specific branding or UI code built *inside* the Client's GHL account shall be owned by the Client upon final payment.

## 4. Revenue Share Verification (Audit Rights)
If a Revenue Share model is active, Client agrees to provide a monthly report (e.g., GHL Smart List export) showing the number of active users with the agreed-upon tag. Provider reserves the right to request a live screen-share audit once per quarter.

## 5. Termination & Deactivation
*   **Non-Payment:** If a Licensing or Rev-Share payment is >7 days late, Provider reserves the right to deactivate all API endpoints and "Smart Features" until the balance is cleared.
*   **Notice:** Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice.
