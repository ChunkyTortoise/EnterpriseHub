**How It Works:**

**Business Impact:**

### **Feature 12: Multi-Channel Sequence Orchestrator** üì± (Practical + AI)

**What It Does:**
Coordinates messages across SMS, Email, Voice, and even Direct Mail in intelligent sequences.

**How It Works:**
1. **Smart Channel Selection**: AI chooses best channel based on:
   - Lead's preferred communication method (learns from history)
   - Message type (urgent = SMS, detailed = email)
   - Time of day (morning = email, evening = SMS)
   - Previous response rates per channel

2. **Dynamic Sequencing**: Adjusts sequence based on engagement:
   - Lead opens email ‚Üí Wait 2 hours ‚Üí Follow-up SMS
   - Lead doesn't open ‚Üí Try different channel
   - Lead responds ‚Üí Stop sequence, notify agent

3. **Cross-Channel Consistency**: Same message, optimized for each channel

**Example Sequence:**
```
Day 1, 9 AM:  Email - "Welcome! Here's what we found for you..."
Day 1, 6 PM:  SMS - "Hi! Did you see the properties I sent this morning?"
Day 2, 10 AM: Email - "Quick question: What's your timeline?"
Day 3, 4 PM:  SMS - "Have 5 min for a quick call?"
Day 5, 12 PM: Voice Drop - "Wanted to personally reach out..."
Day 7, 9 AM:  Break-up Email - "Should I take you off my list?"
```

**AI Optimization:**
- Tests different sequences
- Finds optimal timing per lead
- A/B tests messaging
- Learns from success patterns

**Business Impact:**
- Multi-channel = +60% response rate vs. single channel
- Optimal timing = +25% engagement
- Reduced spam perception (not bombarding one channel)
- Professional, coordinated experience
