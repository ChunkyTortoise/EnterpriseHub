# Sales Cadence (SMB Focus)

## Weekly Targets
- 40 targeted outbound contacts
- 8 qualified conversations
- 2-3 proposals sent

## ICP Filters
- SMB real estate teams (2-20 agents)
- Measurable lead response-time leakage
- Existing CRM usage and willingness to integrate automation

## Revenue Targets
- Proposal->paid close rate >= 20%
- Avg deal size: $4K-$8K
- Month-3 booked revenue: $12K-$20K
