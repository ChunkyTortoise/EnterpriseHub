## DIY vs. EnterpriseHub

If you're technical and have 4+ weeks, DIY gives you full control. EnterpriseHub gives you:
- Production-ready GHL integration (no webhook headaches)
- Battle-tested qualification logic with the Q0-Q4 framework
- Built-in compliance (TCPA opt-out, FHA/RESPA, SB 243 AI disclosure)
- 3-tier caching for 89% token cost reduction
- Ongoing maintenance included

**Choose DIY if**: You have a developer on staff and specific requirements that standard products can't meet.

## Sierra.ai vs. EnterpriseHub

Sierra.ai is a polished enterprise product -- best for large brokerages (50+ agents) with $2K+/month budget. EnterpriseHub is better for:
- Solo agents and small teams (< 10 agents)
- Teams already on GoHighLevel
- Wanting month-to-month flexibility
- Needing multi-bot routing (Lead -> Buyer/Seller handoff with confidence scoring)

## Structurely vs. EnterpriseHub

Structurely is a solid SMS follow-up tool. EnterpriseHub goes further:
- Multi-bot routing (Lead -> Buyer/Seller handoff with 0.7 confidence threshold)
- Full BI dashboard with Monte Carlo simulations, sentiment analysis, and churn detection
- Open source core you can inspect and customize
- Automatic language detection and response matching
- Built-in compliance pipeline (TCPA, FHA/RESPA, California SB 243)
