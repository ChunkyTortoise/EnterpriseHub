## Subject Line A/B Testing Recommendations

**Test 1: Question vs Statement**
- A: "Are your leads going cold while competitors respond in 60 seconds?"
- B: "Your competitors are responding in 60 seconds. Are you?"

**Test 2: Specificity**
- A: "How Premier Real Estate achieved 49,400% ROI"
- B: "From 8% to 24% conversion: The Premier Real Estate story"

**Test 3: Pain vs Gain**
- A: "55% of your leads never get follow-up. Here's how to fix it."
- B: "How to achieve 98% follow-up consistency (without hiring)"

## Follow-Up Response Handling

**If they say**: "Send me more information"
**Response**: "I'll send you three things: (1) Full Premier Real Estate case study, (2) Your customized ROI projection, (3) One-page summary. Which industry-specific example would be most relevant for you?"

**If they say**: "Not interested right now"
**Response**: "Totally understand. Can I ask — is it timing, budget, or just not a current priority? (Helps me know when to follow up)"

**If they say**: "This sounds too good to be true"
**Response**: "I get that reaction a lot — 49,400% ROI is a huge number. That's why I'm offering to connect you with Sarah Chen directly. She'll share the real story, including challenges they faced. Would a 20-minute reference call be valuable?"

**If they say**: "We already have a CRM"
**Response**: "Great! Most of our clients had CRMs before EnterpriseHub. The difference is AI-powered automation vs manual workflows. Premier was using [Common CRM] before switching. Want to see a side-by-side comparison?"
