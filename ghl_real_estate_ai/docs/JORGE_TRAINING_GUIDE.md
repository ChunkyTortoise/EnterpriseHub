### Immediate Intervention Needed:

**Situation 1: Lead is Frustrated**
- Signs: "Stop texting", "Not interested", "Why are you asking so many questions?"
- **Action:** Turn AI off, apologize personally, offer human help

**Situation 2: Lead Has Complex Question**
- Signs: "What's the HOA fee?", "Can I see this property tomorrow?"
- **Action:** AI can't answer these - step in manually

**Situation 3: Lead Scored Hot But Seems Cold**
- Signs: Short answers, no enthusiasm, score doesn't match vibe
- **Action:** Review conversation, adjust your approach

**Situation 4: AI Got Stuck**
- Signs: Same question repeated, no progress after 5+ messages
- **Action:** Turn AI off, start fresh conversation manually
