# EnterpriseHub: AI That Qualifies Your Leads While You Sleep

## What It Does (In Plain English)

EnterpriseHub connects to your GoHighLevel CRM and automatically responds to new leads via SMS -- 24/7, in under 3 minutes. It asks the right qualification questions, figures out if they're serious, and either books an appointment or continues nurturing them -- all without you lifting a finger.

Three AI bots handle different lead types:
- **Lead Bot** -- First contact with new inquiries. Scores interest, sets temperature (hot/warm/cold).
- **Buyer Bot** -- Qualifies buyer readiness: budget, pre-approval status, timeline, preferences.
- **Seller Bot** -- Qualifies seller motivation: timeline, price expectations, property condition.

## How It Works

1. A new lead comes into your GHL account (from Zillow, your website, Facebook ads, etc.)
2. The lead gets the "Needs Qualifying" tag (you set this up once, or your existing workflow adds it)
3. Within minutes, the Lead Bot sends the first SMS -- warm, conversational, and on-brand
4. Over 3-5 messages, the bot qualifies the lead using proven questions
5. Hot leads get routed to the Buyer or Seller bot for deeper qualification
6. When ready, the bot books an appointment on your calendar
7. You get a notification with the full conversation history and qualification score

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Will it sound robotic to my leads?**
A: No. The bots are trained to sound like a knowledgeable assistant, not a robot. They use warm, conversational language and never mention they're AI (except when legally required in California under SB 243).

**Q: What if I already have a VA handling leads?**
A: The AI handles the first 3-5 messages (the boring qualification questions). Your VA or you take over once the lead is warm. It removes grunt work, not human relationships.

**Q: Does it work with my existing GHL workflows?**
A: Yes. It uses tags to trigger and exit -- it won't conflict with anything you already have set up.

**Q: What happens if a lead asks something the bot can't handle?**
A: It escalates to you with a notification. The handoff is smooth -- the bot tells the lead a team member will follow up shortly.

**Q: Is my lead data safe?**
A: All data stays in your GHL account. EnterpriseHub reads and writes to GHL but never stores your lead data on its own servers.

**Q: Can I customize what questions it asks?**
A: Yes. The qualification questions, thresholds, and tone are all configurable. Takes 30 minutes to customize for your market.

**Q: What if I'm in a different market (not Rancho Cucamonga)?**
A: The system is market-agnostic. I'll adapt the prompts and pricing benchmarks to your market during setup.

**Q: Does it handle Spanish-speaking leads?**
A: Yes -- automatic language detection. It responds in the same language the lead messages in.

**Q: What's the minimum contract?**
A: Month-to-month. Cancel anytime. No setup fees.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: For most GHL accounts: 1-2 hours. I do the technical work; you just grant access.
