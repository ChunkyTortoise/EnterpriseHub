## Use Case Examples

### Example 1: Facebook Ads Optimization
The team thought Facebook was their best performer. Attribution revealed:

- **Reality**: Facebook drove awareness, but Google drove closes
- **Insight**: 3-touch "assisted" model showed Facebook was actually 40% of the journey
- **Action**: Rebalanced spend; added retargeting
- **Result**: Cost per closing dropped from $890 to $312

### Example 2: Predictive Lead Scoring
Using predictive analytics, the system identified that leads with these traits converted at 4x average:

- ‚úì Visited property 3+ times
- ‚úì Requested info on schools in the area
- ‚úì Viewed mortgage calculator
- ‚úì Returned within 7 days

**Result**: Agents prioritized these leads first, increasing close rate from 12% to 31%.

### Example 3: Churn Detection & Retention
The team was losing 23% of past clients to other agents annually.

- **Detection**: System identified clients showing "risk signals" (no engagement in 60 days, visited competitor sites)
- **Intervention**: Automated nurture sequences + agent check-ins
- **Result**: Reduced churn to 9%, saving $180K in lost future business

## Marketing Attribution Models Explained

Not sure which model is right for you? Here's a quick guide:

| Model | Best For | How It Works |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **First Touch** | Brand awareness | Credits first interaction |
| **Last Touch** | Direct response | Credits final interaction |
| **Linear** | Balanced | Equal credit to all touches |
| **Time Decay** | Short sales cycles | More credit to recent touches |
| **Position Based** | Complex journeys | 40% first, 40% last, 20% middle |
| **Algorithmic** | Maximum accuracy | AI determines credit based on actual impact |

insight-engine lets you compare all models simultaneously to find what works best for your business.
