## 2. COMPENSATION STRUCTURE

### **2.1 Recommended: Project Portfolio Model**

**Structure**: Pure project-based compensation with performance bonuses
**No Monthly Retainer**: Pay only for completed deliverables

**Core Project Pricing:**
- AI Integration Projects: $4,000-8,000/project
- Lead Intelligence Features: $3,000-5,000/project
- Automation Workflows: $2,000-4,000/project
- Enterprise Features: $5,000-8,000/project
- Platform Optimization: $1,500-3,000/project

**Performance Bonuses:**
- Customer acquisition: $1,000 per new enterprise customer
- Revenue growth: 5% of monthly revenue increases you create (capped at $1,000/month)
- Performance improvements: $2,000 for major KPI achievements
- Milestone bonuses: $3,000 (at $25K MRR), $6,000 (at $50K MRR), $12,000 (at $100K MRR)

**Payment Schedule:**
- 30% upon project start
- 50% at milestone completion
- 20% upon final delivery and acceptance

### **2.2 Alternative: Small Retainer + Projects**

**Monthly Retainer**: $2,000/month
- Priority response (24-48 hours)
- Monthly strategy consultation (90 minutes)
- Minor bug fixes and adjustments
- Platform monitoring and recommendations

**Project Pricing**: 15% discount on standard rates
- AI Integration Projects: $3,400-6,800/project
- Feature Development: $1,300-4,250/project
- Strategic Consulting Projects: $1,500-3,500/project

**Revenue Share**: 5% of incremental MRR (capped at $1,500/month)
- Only applies to features YOU build that directly generate revenue
- 18-month term with 6-month evaluation periods

### **2.3 Alternative: Results-Based Performance Model**

**Base Project Fees**: 70% of standard rates
**Example**: $4,200 base for $6,000 AI integration project

**Performance Multipliers:**
- Exceeds performance targets: +30% bonus
- Customer satisfaction >4.5/5: +20% bonus
- Delivers ahead of schedule: +15% bonus
- Generates measurable ROI: +25% bonus

**Success Milestones:**
- $25K MRR achievement: $3,000 bonus
- $50K MRR achievement: $6,000 bonus
- $100K MRR achievement: $15,000 bonus

**Annual Compensation Cap**: $120,000 total
