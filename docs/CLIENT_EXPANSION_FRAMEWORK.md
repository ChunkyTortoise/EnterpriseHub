### Strategic Priority Scoring (1-10)

**Formula**:
```
Priority Score = (
  Revenue_Weight * (Expansion_Potential / $5K) +
  Probability_Weight * (Success_Probability * 10) +
  Readiness_Weight * (Readiness_Score / 10)
```

**Weights**:
- Revenue: 40%

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Potential Risks

**1. Client Resistance**
- **Risk**: Price sensitivity, timing concerns
- **Mitigation**: ROI-based pricing justification, flexible payment terms, early adopter discounts

**2. Implementation Capacity**
- **Risk**: Cannot deliver on sold contracts
- **Mitigation**: Stagger implementations, hire contractors, prioritize quick wins

**3. Competitive Response**
- **Risk**: Competitors offering lower prices
- **Mitigation**: Value differentiation, lock-in long-term contracts, demonstrate ROI

**4. Economic Downturn**
- **Risk**: Budget freezes, delayed decisions
- **Mitigation**: Emphasize efficiency gains, offer performance-based pricing

**5. Sales Execution**
- **Risk**: Team cannot close at required velocity
- **Mitigation**: Sales coaching, commission incentives, fractional closer support
