### EnterpriseHub: Real Estate AI Transformation

**Client:** Mid-market Real Estate Agency  
**Impact:** 87% reduction in manual review time | $240,000 annual savings

**The Challenge:**
Manual lead qualification was consuming 20+ hours/week with inconsistent quality. The client lacked predictive analytics for conversion probability and had spiraling token costs.

**The Solution:**
A production-grade multi-agent AI system orchestrating Lead, Buyer, and Seller bots with intelligent handoff workflows.
- **Architecture:** Microservices with FastAPI, Redis, and PostgreSQL.
- **AI Stack:** Claude, Gemini, Perplexity with LangChain orchestration.
- **Performance:** Three-tier caching (L1/L2/L3) achieving 89% token cost reduction.

**Key Outcomes:**
- **Manual Review Reduced by 87%**: From 20+ hrs/week to <3 hrs/week.
- **$240,000 Annual Savings**: Through automation and cost optimization.
- **P95 Latency <2s**: Ensuring rapid response times for hot leads.
- **3x Qualified Leads**: Driven by predictive scoring models.

> *"The multi-agent system transformed our lead qualification process. We went from 20+ hours of manual work per week to under 3 hours, while actually improving lead quality."* ‚Äî Operations Manager
