# Project Context: EnterpriseHub

## Overview
EnterpriseHub is an enterprise-grade Real Estate AI Platform designed for high-stakes market analysis, lead intelligence, and autonomous business automation. It serves as a professional showcase for specialized AI services, specifically targeting the real estate and FinTech sectors.

## Core Mission
To transform specialized real estate expertise into monetizable AI assets through autonomous swarm orchestration and high-fidelity analytics.

## Key Stakeholders
- **Primary Client:** Jorge Salas / Lyrio.io
- **Lead Developer:** Cayman Roden
- **Target Audience:** Real Estate PE firms, Investors, Agency owners.

## Strategic Position
EnterpriseHub utilizes a "Modular Monolith" architecture with a specialized multi-agent swarm ("Strike Team Beta") to parallelize complex tasks such as market analysis, content generation, and technical due diligence.

## Core Capabilities
1. **Virtual AI Architect:** Autonomous strategy lead-intake.
2. **Technical Due Diligence:** Rigorous audit framework for AI valuation.
3. **Business Automation (S6):** Interactive workflow simulator for autonomous enterprise processes.
4. **ROI Lab:** Multi-vertical financial models with verified math logic.
5. **Market Pulse:** Institutional-grade technical analysis dashboard.
6. **Margin Hunter:** Consultant-grade CVP and profitability modeling.
