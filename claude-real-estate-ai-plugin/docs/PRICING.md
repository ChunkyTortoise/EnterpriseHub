# Pricing & Licensing Strategy

**Product**: Claude Real Estate AI Accelerator
**Updated**: January 15, 2026
**Version**: 1.0

## Executive Summary

Three-tier pricing model designed to:
1. **Maximize adoption** through generous free tier
2. **Convert serious users** to affordable Pro tier
3. **Capture enterprise value** with premium tier
4. **Generate consulting revenue** from custom development

**Target Metrics** (Year 1):
- 5,000+ free tier users
- 500+ Pro tier conversions (10% conversion rate)
- 25+ Enterprise customers
- $50k+ consulting revenue

## Upgrade & Downgrade Policy

### Upgrading

**Free → Pro**:
- Instant activation
- Immediate access to all 27 skills
- Charged full annual price

**Pro → Enterprise**:
- Prorated credit for remaining Pro time
- Immediate access to Enterprise features
- Charged difference only

### Downgrading

**Enterprise → Pro**:
- Allowed at annual renewal
- Prorated refund of difference
- Enterprise features disabled immediately

**Pro → Free**:
- Allowed anytime
- Prorated refund of remaining time
- Advanced skills locked immediately
