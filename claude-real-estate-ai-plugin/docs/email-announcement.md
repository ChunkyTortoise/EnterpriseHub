## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use numbers when possible
- Create urgency (limited time, last day)
- Personalize with [First Name] when available
- A/B test every campaign

### Content
- Get to the point in first 2 sentences
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)
- Include visuals (screenshots, GIFs)
- One clear call-to-action per email
- Mobile-optimized (70% of opens are mobile)

### Timing
- **Best days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times**: 10 AM, 2 PM, 6 PM (recipient's timezone)
- **Avoid**: Weekends, Mondays before 10 AM, Fridays after 3 PM

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate (target: 25-35%)
- Click-through rate (target: 3-5%)
- Conversion rate (target: 1-3%)
- Unsubscribe rate (keep under 0.5%)
