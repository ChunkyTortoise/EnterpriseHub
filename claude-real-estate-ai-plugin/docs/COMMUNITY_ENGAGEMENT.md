## Community Champions Program

**Goal**: Identify and empower 20 community advocates

**Benefits for Champions**:
- Free lifetime Pro tier license
- Early access to all new features (1 month before general release)
- Direct line to product team (private Slack/Discord)
- Featured on website "Community Champions" page
- Exclusive swag packages (t-shirts, stickers, notebook)
- Annual "Champion Summit" (virtual or in-person)
- Revenue share for referrals (10% of first year)

**Expectations**:
- Create 1 tutorial/case study per quarter
- Respond to community questions weekly
- Provide product feedback monthly
- Advocate authentically (not marketing copy)

**Selection Criteria**:
- Active community participation (Discord, GitHub, social media)
- High-quality contributions (tutorials, bug reports, PRs)
- Positive attitude and helpfulness
- Real-world plugin usage (not just theoretical)

**Nomination Process**:
- Self-nomination via form
- Team reviews quarterly
- Top 20 selected based on criteria
- Announced publicly with celebration

## Metrics & Analytics

### Community Health Dashboard

**Track Weekly**:
- Discord: New members, active members, messages per day
- GitHub: Stars, forks, issues opened/closed, PR rate
- Reddit: Mentions, upvotes, comment engagement
- Twitter: Followers, mentions, engagement rate
- YouTube: Subscribers, views, watch time
- Blog: Pageviews, time on page, newsletter signups

**Success Indicators**:
- Growing membership (10%+ month-over-month)
- High engagement (30%+ of members active monthly)
- Positive sentiment (90%+ positive mentions)
- User-generated content (5+ per week)
- Referral traffic (15%+ of new users from community)

**Red Flags**:
- Declining engagement (20%+ drop)
- Negative sentiment spike (20%+ negative mentions)
- Slow response times (>24 hours average)
- Low conversion (< 5% free to pro)
- Churn increases (> 20% annual)
